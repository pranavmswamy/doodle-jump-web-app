Doodle Jump Game

A platforming video game developed and published by American studio Lima Sky.

Aim is to guide a four-legged creature called "The Doodler" up a never-ending series of platforms without falling.

Players can get boost from various objects, such as propeller hats, jetpacks, rockets, springs, trampolines.







Game Video







Goal

Build an AI player model that interacts with the game and allows the doodler to climb up the platforms.

Explore a combination of computer vision and reinforcement learning agents like DQN, DRQN, PPO and A3C.

Attack the model using Adversarial Attack
